## Why
When work is done, the builder needs a clear overview of what the next sensible steps
should be — surfaced as a clear output rather than left implicit in the conversation.

## What
Design a process that, when work completes, produces a clear "what next" overview of the
next sensible steps. Two candidate shapes to consider: (a) a new agent or skill that runs
to generate the overview, or (b) an analysis of the conversation with the worker. The
deliverable is the design of this process.
